About the Studio

Crafting the
impossible
frame by frame.

Devansh Edits is a premier VFX studio built on a singular obsession: making the impossible look inevitable. Founded by a VFX veteran with credits spanning major Hollywood productions, global OTT originals, and award-winning commercials, we bring cinematic-grade visual effects to every frame.

From photorealistic environment extensions to creature animation, particle simulation to seamless compositing — every pixel is intentional, every frame a work of art.

What We Do

Full-spectrum
VFX services.

Visual Compositing

Seamlessly integrate CG elements with live-action footage. 2D/3D compositing using industry-standard Nuke and After Effects pipelines for Hollywood-level results.

3D VFX & Animation

Photorealistic 3D assets, environment creation, character rigging, and simulation. Fluids, smoke, fire, destruction — all crafted with Houdini and Cinema 4D.

Motion Graphics

Cinematic title sequences, broadcast graphics, and branded motion content. Frame-perfect animation that elevates every project from opening card to final frame.

Color Grading

Cinematic color science and DI grading using DaVinci Resolve. From raw footage to a polished, unified visual language — every look is tailored to your story.

Matte Painting

Photorealistic digital matte paintings for set extensions, period environments, and fantastical worlds. Hyperdetailed artistry that places your story anywhere imaginable.

Full Film VFX

End-to-end VFX supervision and production for feature films, OTT series, and commercials. On-set data capture to final delivery — complete cinematic solutions.

How It Works

A seamless
creative process.

01
Discovery

We analyze your script, storyboards, and creative vision. Shot-by-shot briefing to align on look, feel, and technical requirements before a single frame is touched.

02
Pre-Viz

Concept artwork, 3D pre-visualization, and style frames are produced. You see exactly how every shot will look before production begins.

03
Production

Full VFX production with regular WIP updates every 48 hours. You're always in the loop — no black-box surprises, total creative transparency.

04
Delivery

Polished final renders in your required format and codec. Full quality check, color-managed delivery, and post-delivery support for broadcast/streaming compliance.
